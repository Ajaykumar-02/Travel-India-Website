Our Projects



Tritiy Yatra

_________


Embark on a spiritual journey across India's sacred destinations — from the ghats of Varanasi to the heights of Kedarnath.









Snow Adventure

_________


Embrace the magic of India’s winter wonderlands! From skiing and snowboarding in Gulmarg to thrilling snow trekking in Ladakh and Himachal, our snow adventures offer heart-pounding fun and breathtaking views. Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, get ready to explore the serene beauty and adrenaline rush of snowy mountains.









Camping

_________


Reconnect with nature by camping in some of India’s most beautiful and serene landscapes. Whether it’s desert camping in Rajasthan, forest camps in Uttarakhand, or lakeside spots in Kerala, experience the joy of sleeping under the stars, sharing stories by the campfire, and waking up to breathtaking sunrises.









Water Sports

_________


Dive into thrilling water sports across India’s stunning lakes, rivers, and coastlines. Whether it’s white-water rafting in Rishikesh, kayaking through Kerala’s backwaters, or surfing the waves in Goa, our water adventures are perfect for both beginners and adrenaline junkies looking to make a splash!
